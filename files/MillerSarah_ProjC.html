USER INSTRUCTIONS

This is an interactive 3D world.
You can nagivagte the scene, manipulate several objects, control the lighting, and try different shading modes.

Navigation
To walk through the scene, use the arrow keys
To angle the camera, use the WASD keys

Object Manipulation
Click and drag to manipulate the star
Press O and P to remove and add flower petals

Lighting
You are wearing a headlight! While it is on, there will always be light in the direction you are looking. To toggle, press "h".
To control the main lighting, use these controls, and the space bar to flip the light on and off entirely!
Ambient light:
R: G: B:
Light position:
X: Y: Z:
Diffuse light color:
R: G: B:
Specular light color:
R: G: B:

To swap between classic "Blin-Phong" lighting, and crazy noir inspired lighting, press "l".

Shading Modes
To swap between different "materials" and classic individually colored vertices, press "m".
