USER INSTRUCTIONS

You are wearing a headlight! While it is on, there will always be light in the direction you are looking. To turn it on/off, press "h".

To navigate the scene, use the arrow keys
To angle the camera, use the WASD keys

Click and drag to manipulate the star
Press O and P to remove and add flower petals

You can control a light!
Just use these boxes, and the space bar to flip the light on and off entirely!
Ambient light:
R: G: B:
Light position:
X: Y: Z:
Diffuse light color:
R: G: B:
Specular light color:
R: G: B:

To swap between classic "Blin-Phong" lighting, and crazy noir inspired lighting, press "l".

To swap between different "materials" and classic individually colored vertices, press "m".
